Data-Driven Polymer Interaction Chromatography

Making polymer separations predictable and reproducible

PolyIC is an open platform to design, understand, and simulate polymer interaction chromatography (PIC). It brings together shared data, practical knowledge, and predictive tools to make PIC easier to use and easier to reproduce.
