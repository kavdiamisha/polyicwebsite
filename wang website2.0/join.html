Community

Join the PolyIC Network

PolyIC grows through community contributions. Whether you are an academic researcher, industry scientist, or student, there are several ways to get involved and benefit from the network.

Ways to Contribute

📤

Submit Data

Contribute experimental IC records to PolyCrit. All submissions are reviewed and credited to your research group.

🔍

Peer Review

Join the PolyCrit data review panel to help validate submitted records and maintain database quality.

🤝

Collaborate

Partner with the PolyIC group on joint publications, grant proposals, or method development projects.

🎓

Join the Lab

Graduate and postdoctoral positions are available. Contact Dr. Wang with your CV and research interests.

For Industry Partners

Industry researchers can contribute anonymized experimental data, access the full PolyCrit database, and engage with the advisory board. Contact us to discuss partnership options.

Stay Updated

Sign up for the PolyIC newsletter to receive updates on new database releases, publications, and events.
